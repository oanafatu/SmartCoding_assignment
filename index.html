Some statistics of the city

  • As of 2011 census data, Baia Mare has a population of 123.738, a decrease from the figure recorded at the 2002 census.
  • The ethnic composition of the city is as follows:
    1. Romanian: (84.11%)
    2. Hungarian: (12.25%)
    3. Roma: (2.76%)
    4. Germans: (0.24%)
    5. Ukrainian: (0.16%)
    6. Jews: (0.02%)
  • Baia Mare is a municipality along the Săsar River, in northwestern Romania; it is the capital of Maramureș County. The city is situated about 600 kilometres (373 miles) from Bucharest, 70 km (43 mi) from the border with Hungary, and 50 km (31 mi) from the border with Ukraine.
